Kalpitiya - Bar Reef Resort

Set in a quaint village in Sri Lanka's Puttalam District, this rustic beachfront resort overlooks the Laccadive Sea and is 9 km from Talawila Church. Simple cottages with mud walls, thatched roofs and handcrafted coconut palm decor also feature free Wi-Fi and open-air bathrooms in walled gardens. The 2 villas feature 2 bedrooms and sofabeds, plus waterfall showers and private verandahs. Mosquito nets are provided. Room service is available. There are 2 eateries with a bohemian vibe, and an outdoor pool overlooking the sea. Water sports, whale/dolphin watching and snorkelling can be arranged. Parking is free

Address: Palmyrah Avenue,Norochcholoai,Alankuda, Off Kalpitiya Road,Sri Lanka
