Passikudah - Maalu Maalu Resort

Occupying a series of low-rise buildings with thatched roofs, this relaxed resort is right on Pasikuda Beach. Valaichchenai Lagoon is 11 km away. Funky chalet rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and balconies, plus air-conditioning, minifridges and tea and coffeemaking facilities. They also have rainfall showers; some have loft areas for kids. Suites add Jacuzzis, pools and steam rooms. Yoga and meditation classes are free. Other amenities include an outdoor pool and a spa. There are 2 elegant restaurants; 1 specialises in seafood and the other is on the beach; there's also a poolside bar/restaurant made from converted fishing boats.
