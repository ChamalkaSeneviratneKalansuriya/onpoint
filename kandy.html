Kandy - Cinnamon Citadel

Overlooking the Mahaweli River, this upscale hotel in a modern building is 4 km from the Kandy city centre and the Sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, and 8 km from the Peradeniya Royal Botanic Gardens. The airy, stylish rooms have minibars, cable TV and free Wi-Fi, plus garden or river views. Upgraded rooms have balconies and living areas, and suites add living rooms, mountain views and extra bathrooms. Amenities include an airy restaurant with a terrace, a polished bar with regular live music, and a warm, romantic restaurant with river views. There's also an outdoor pool and a fitness centre, along with gardens and BBQ facilities..
